# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Growmax handles user information.

Growmax follows a data-minimal approach.

## Information Collected

Growmax does not collect:

- Names
- Emails
- Phone numbers
- Passwords

Information may include:

- Wallet addresses
- On-chain transaction data
- Technical usage metrics

## Use of Information

Collected information is used to:

- Operate platform functionality
- Ensure security and integrity
- Monitor system performance

Growmax does not sell user data.

## Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are applied to protect platform data.

However, no system guarantees absolute security.

## Third-Party Services

Growmax may rely on third-party infrastructure providers.

These providers are subject to their own privacy practices.
