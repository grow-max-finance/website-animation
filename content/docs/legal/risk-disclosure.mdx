# Risk Disclosure

This section describes the risks associated with using Growmax products.

Digital asset participation involves inherent risks. Users should evaluate these risks carefully before engaging.

## Market Risk

Digital asset prices can be volatile.

Risks include:

- Sudden price movements
- Market illiquidity
- Adverse price execution

Borrow positions may be liquidated if collateral values decline.

## Smart Contract & Technology Risk

Growmax relies on blockchain infrastructure and smart contracts.

Risks include:

- Software bugs
- Network congestion
- Protocol-level vulnerabilities

While precautions are taken, no system is entirely risk-free.

## Custody & Operational Risk

During product participation:

- Assets may be held under controlled custody
- Withdrawals are subject to platform rules
- Operational failures or security incidents may affect access or execution

## Liquidation Risk (Borrow)

Borrowing involves the risk of:

- Margin calls
- Forced liquidation
- Partial or total collateral loss

Liquidation is automated and cannot be reversed.

## Regulatory & Legal Risk

Digital asset regulations continue to evolve.

Changes in laws may:

- Affect product availability
- Restrict platform access
- Require operational changes

Growmax may adapt its services accordingly.

## No Assurance of Outcomes

Past results or stated reward rates do not guarantee future outcomes.

Rewards depend on platform operation, market conditions, and adherence to rules.
