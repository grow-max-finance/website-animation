# Deposits & Network Rules

All deposits on Growmax are user-initiated and wallet-confirmed.

## Deposit Rules

Users must ensure:

- The blockchain selected is **BNB Chain**
- The token standard is **BEP-20**
- The asset is supported for the selected product

**Growmax does not auto-detect or convert deposits.**

## Processing & Confirmation

Deposits are:

- Confirmed on-chain
- Reflected after sufficient BNB Chain confirmations
- Subject to network congestion and block finality

Processing times may vary.

## No Manual Recovery

Growmax does not:

- Recover incorrect deposits
- Bridge assets from other chains
- Convert unsupported tokens

**Users bear full responsibility for deposit accuracy.**

## Important Warnings

- Always verify the network is BNB Chain before depositing
- Always verify the token standard is BEP-20
- Always verify the asset is supported
- Deposits to wrong networks cannot be recovered
- Deposits of unsupported tokens cannot be recovered

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- Selecting the correct network (BNB Chain)
- Using the correct token standard (BEP-20)
- Verifying asset support before depositing
- Understanding that incorrect deposits may be permanently lost
