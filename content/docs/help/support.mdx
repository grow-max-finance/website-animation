### Common Issues

**Transaction Issues:**
- Verify you're using BNB Chain
- Ensure you have sufficient BNB for gas fees
- Check transaction status on BNB Chain explorer

**Deposit Issues:**
- Confirm you're using BEP-20 tokens
- Verify the asset is supported
- Wait for network confirmations

**Withdrawal Issues:**
- Ensure you meet withdrawal eligibility requirements
- Verify you have sufficient balance
- Check that you're withdrawing to the correct wallet

## When Contacting Support

To help us assist you quickly, please include:

1. **Your wallet address** (never share private keys)
2. **Transaction hash** (if applicable)
3. **Detailed description** of the issue
4. **Screenshots** (if relevant)
5. **Steps you've already taken** to resolve the issue

## Important Security Reminders

- **Never share your private keys or seed phrase** with anyone
- Growmax support will **never ask for your private keys**
- Be cautious of impersonators on social media
- Always verify you're communicating through official channels
- Enable two-factor authentication on your wallet if available
